Many online archives allow 1 Gig of free space freely accessible to add and delete material in very easy to use
web interfaces which you can access over the internet. We highly recommend Google Drive as an email address with
Youtube Channel and Google Site (responsive to all devices) also available with their own web interfaces.

Subject matter material must be sourced and formatted in the Preliminary process before being archived.
After documents and imagery have been deposited into an archive they must be shared with the appropriate access
permissions to be made available for further use in the Rational and Visual processes that will reference them.
